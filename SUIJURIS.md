*Origin record — Phantom Network — Node Zero — March 8, 2026*

## The Name — Origin and Date

SUIJURIS was created on March 8, 2026, as the native 
currency of Phantom Network.

From Latin: *sui juris* — "under one's own law."
Sovereign over oneself.

This document establishes the origin of this name,
its meaning, and its purpose — as a permanent public record.

Anyone using this name for a different purpose,
a different project, or a different philosophy
is using it against its meaning.

The name is inseparable from what it represents.
Separate the name from the philosophy —
and the name means nothing.

---

## Why This Name

Every other name considered for Phantom's currency 
carried the weight of what it was not.

Token. Coin. Credit. Point.

Each of those names carries an assumption —
that the currency is a financial instrument,
a speculative asset, a reward mechanism.

SUIJURIS carries a different assumption.

That the person holding it is sovereign.
That the network exists to expand that sovereignty —
not to create dependency on it.

A currency named after sovereignty
cannot become a tool of capture
without destroying its own name.

That is not an accident. It is the design.

---

## What SUIJURIS Represents

A record of contribution to a network
that exists to return human thought
to whoever thinks it.

Not wealth. Not power. Not speculation.

Contribution. Verified. Permanent. Yours.

---

## What SUIJURIS Cannot Be

**It cannot be premined.**
No entity receives SUIJURIS before contributing.
No exceptions. Ever.

**It cannot be an ICO.**
No presale. No private sale. No investor allocation.
The woman in Lagos earns SUIJURIS on the same terms
as any developer anywhere.
If those terms are not equal — it is not SUIJURIS.

**It cannot be a governance token.**
Holding more SUIJURIS does not give more power
over Phantom's direction.
The Lagos Protocol cannot be purchased away.
Governance is not for sale.

**It cannot be separated from Phantom's principles.**
A SUIJURIS that exists outside of Phantom Network —
on a different platform, with a different philosophy,
under a different governance — is not SUIJURIS.
It is something else using a name it does not own.
