# Economics — SUIJURIS

*Phantom Network — Node Zero — March 8, 2026*

---

## Status

SUIJURIS does not exist yet.

This document describes what it is designed to be,
why it needs to exist, and what it must never become.

Honesty about what is not built yet is part of 
Phantom's architecture. A project that describes 
unbuilt systems as existing is a project that 
has already begun to drift.

---

## The Name

*Sui juris* — Latin. "Under one's own law."

Sovereign over oneself.

The name is not decoration. It is the design principle.
A currency that makes its holders more dependent 
on others is not SUIJURIS. It has failed its name.

---

## Why an Economic Layer

Phantom runs on devices. Devices cost money.
Bandwidth costs money. Storage costs money.
Development costs money.

A network that depends entirely on altruism 
will not reach the woman in Lagos.
Altruism does not scale. Aligned incentives do.

SUIJURIS exists to make honest contribution 
more profitable than exploitation.
Not through enforcement — through architecture.

The network is healthier when more nodes contribute.
Healthier networks are worth more to every node.
Your individual interest is mathematically aligned 
with collective wellbeing.

That is the design. Not a promise of virtue.
A structure that makes virtue the rational choice.

---

## What SUIJURIS Is

A record of contribution to the network.

You earn SUIJURIS by contributing:

**Compute** — running AI inference for other nodes
that need processing power they do not have locally.

**Storage** — preserving sealed ideas and collective 
memory that the network needs to persist.

**Translation** — converting complex knowledge into 
forms accessible to nodes without technical background.
The person who explains the sealing system in Yoruba 
is contributing something the network needs.
SUIJURIS recognizes that contribution.

**Verification** — running independent checks that 
keep the network honest.

**Development** — building components the network needs.

---

## What SUIJURIS Is Not

**Not a speculative asset.**
SUIJURIS represents contribution made.
Not a bet on future value.
Anyone treating it as a speculative instrument 
is using it against its purpose.

**Not a fundraising mechanism.**
No ICO. No presale. No private sale.
No entity receives tokens before contributing.

**Not a control mechanism.**
Holding more SUIJURIS does not give more governance power.
Governance is not for sale.
The Lagos Protocol cannot be purchased away.

**Not a founder reward.**
The founding allocation is public and immutable.
It reflects the contribution of building the genesis.
It does not reflect ownership of the organism.
No one owns the organism.

---

## Fair Launch

The only way SUIJURIS enters existence 
is through contribution to the network.

No premining.
No founder allocation beyond what is publicly documented.
No investor allocation.
No exceptions.

The woman in Lagos who contributes her device's 
idle compute earns SUIJURIS on the same terms 
as any developer anywhere.

If those terms are not equal — it is not Phantom.

---

## The Honest Questions

SUIJURIS has not been designed in full detail yet.
These questions remain open:

*How is contribution measured fairly across 
different types of nodes with different capabilities?*

*How does the network prevent gaming —
nodes that appear to contribute without actually doing so?*

*How does SUIJURIS remain stable enough to be useful
without becoming speculative?*

*What prevents early nodes from accumulating 
disproportionate SUIJURIS and gaining de facto control?*

These are not rhetorical questions.
They are the design work that remains.

Anyone who arrives with genuine answers 
is contributing something Phantom needs.

---

## What Comes Next

SUIJURIS will be designed when node-to-node 
communication exists.

An economic layer built before the network exists 
is speculation about a network that has not been tested.
The architecture must inform the economics —
not the other way around.

When the first two nodes communicate through Phantom —
the design of SUIJURIS begins in earnest.

Until then — this document holds the principles.
The principles do not change.
The implementation will follow them.
