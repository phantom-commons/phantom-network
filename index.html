Principles
Privacy by architecture, not by policy. No central authority. Universal access. Radical transparency. Founders become obsolete by design. The organism evolves — the principles do not.
Vision
What Phantom is growing toward
Sealed thoughts that travel between devices without internet. Proposals and votes as verifiable seals. An economic layer (SUIJURIS) where contribution is the only currency. Local AI that runs on your device — no query leaves, no company sees your questions. A network that feeds itself.
What Phantom will not become
A social network. A startup. A tool for those who already have privacy. If the woman in Lagos with a secondhand phone cannot use it — it is not Phantom.
